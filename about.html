About Me

This is what I do.

Experience

I began taking lessons at the age of 7, and as soon as I learned my first song I realized that piano was one of my passions. I found that the more I learned about music theory, sight reading, expressions, all the components of music, I naturally wanted to begin writing music. Over the years of practicing and working hard to master and perform songs, I have learned a lot about persistance and hard work. It has translated directly into other areas of my life, and have seen a lot of benefits as a result of my learning how to play the piano.

With the piano that I have learned, I have taken the time to share my talents with others, which I believe is an important part of giving back. A few of the areas that I've been able to share are:

  • Senior Centers
  • Jon Schmidt Concerts
  • Religious Events
  • Writing and recording music
One of the expectations we have in my lesson studio is that we will give back. There will be many opportunities to share what you learn with others, and as we practice sharing naturally all will improve at their performing skills which is one of the most valuable skills that can be taught by a piano teacher.

Once you have taken lessons with me for about a year, you will have been introduced to:

  1. Beethoven,
  2. Mozart,
  3. Lizst,
  4. Chopin,
  5. Joplin,
  6. Debussy,
  7. and other classical artists.
